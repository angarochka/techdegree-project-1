Cat named Koshka

Story

The cat came into the family 9 years ago. I found her at the entrance of my house in December, coming home late at night. Without any hesitation, I took her in. I searched for her owners for a couple of weeks, and in the end, I was very happy that no owner was found.

She has been a full member of the family for 9 years now. I have changed cities three times in the last 5 years, and my pet has always been with me.

Some facts about Koshka

  • She goes crazy for crisps and pistachios.

  • She is tiny even thoug she has a good appetite. Her weight has not exceeded 2.3kg in the last 7 years.

  • Her two front legs have metal elements as a result of surgery after she jumped from the 7th floor while chasing a bird.

  • The cat loves my husband more than me, even though I feed her every day and renew her litter box.

  • "Like any cat, she likes to bask in the sun and always stay nearby to watch.

  • She is very talkative and loves to communicate with us.
