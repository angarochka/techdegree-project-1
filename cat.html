Cat named Koshka

Story

The cat came into the family 9 years ago. I found her in the entrance of my house in December, coming home late at night. Without a second thought I took her in, I searched for her owners for a couple of weeks and in the end I was very happy that no owner was found.

She has been a full member of the family for 9 years now. I have changed three cities in the last 5 years, my pet has always been with me. Because of her great curiosity she unfortunately fell 2 times from the 7th floor, but everything ended up fine.

Some facts about the cat

  • the cat goes crazy for crisps and pistachios.

  • she is tiny even thoug she has a good appetite. Her weight has not exceeded 2.3kg in the last 7 years.

  • The cat is missing three teeth

  • two front legs have metal elements as a result of surgery after jumping after a bird from the 7th floor.

  • The cat loves my husband more than me, even though I feed her every day and renew her litter box.

  • Like any cat, she likes to bask in the sun and always be nearby to watch.
